GPIO Pins for Screen Displays

Component			Pin (GPIO)	Why this pin?

Audio TX (to DF-Player RX)	17		Hardware Serial2 (avoids USB/Boot issues).
Audio RX (from DF-Player TX)	16		Hardware Serial2

GPIO Pins for Regular Inputs

ON/OFF Switch			4		Free pin
Reset/Start Game		5		Free pin

Attack Joystick	Pins		15		Read X voltage
				7		Read Y voltage

Wiring for Accel/RE		9		Shared by all
and LCDs			8 		physical devices	

Data pins cannot all be routed to the same pin
