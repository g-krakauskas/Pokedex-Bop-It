GPIO Pins for Screen Displays

Component			Pin (GPIO)	Why this pin?

Audio TX (to DF-Player RX)	18		Hardware Serial2 (avoids USB/Boot issues).
Audio RX (from DF-Player TX)	17		Hardware Serial2

GPIO Pins for Regular Inputs

ON/OFF Switch			6		Free pin
Reset/Start Game		21		Free pin

Attack Joystick	Pins		4		Read X voltage
				7		Read Y voltage

Wiring for Encoder		8		SDA
and LCDs			9		SCL	

Accel I2C pins			15		SDA
				16		SCL
