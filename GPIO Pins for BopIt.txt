GPIO Pins for Screen Displays

Component			Pin (GPIO)	Why this pin?

Shared SPI Clock (SCLK)		12		Standard SPI clock.
Shared SPI Data (MOSI)		11		Standard SPI data.
Shared Data/Command (DC)	10		Standard for TFTs.
Game Screen CS			13		Dedicated select for Level/Score.
Cosmetic Screen CS		14		Dedicated select for Pokemon Sprites.
Shared Reset (RST)		9		Resets both screens at once to save pins.
Audio TX (to DFPlayer RX)	17		Hardware Serial2 (avoids USB/Boot issues).
Audio RX (from DFPlayer TX)	16		Hardware Serial2.
